# ZURU Melon: Company Policies

**Effective Date:** October 29, 2025
**Last Updated:** October 29, 2025

## Company Overview

At ZURU Melon, we are dedicated to empowering organizations by delivering innovative artificial intelligence (AI) solutions tailored to resolve complex and unique business challenges. We exist to make our clients’ operations smarter, faster, and more future-ready.

### Mission Statement

To enrich businesses by providing exceptional AI-powered tools and services, ensuring client success and ongoing innovation.

## 1. Code of Conduct

### 1.1 Integrity

All employees must act with honesty, fairness, and transparency in every interaction—whether with clients, partners, or colleagues.

### 1.2 Confidentiality

Handling of client data and proprietary information must comply with industry best practices and legal regulations. Employees must:

- Sign and adhere to non-disclosure agreements (NDAs).
- Use confidential information only as required for business purposes.
- Report any data breaches or suspicious activity immediately to the Data Protection Officer.

## 2. AI Ethics & Responsible Use

- All AI solutions must be designed, developed, and deployed ethically.
- Products must not perpetuate bias or discrimination. Regular audits for fairness, accountability, and transparency are mandatory.
- Employees must report any ethical concerns with client use cases to their supervisor or the Ethics Committee.

## 3. Workplace Environment

### 3.1 Diversity & Inclusion

ZURU Melon is committed to fostering a workplace that values diversity, equity, and inclusion. Discrimination or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

### 3.2 Remote & Flexible Work

Employees are encouraged to work in a manner that ensures high productivity and client satisfaction. Remote work is permitted for most roles, subject to project needs and manager approval.

## 4. Client Interaction

- Always represent ZURU Melon professionally.
- Communicate clearly, promptly, and respectfully.
- Never over-promise on capabilities or delivery timelines.
- All client requests for customizations or new features must be reviewed by the Solutions Team.

## 5. Data Security and Privacy

- Use secure channels for data transmission.
- Encrypt sensitive information.
- Access to client data is limited to authorized personnel.
- Employees must complete annual cybersecurity training.

## 6. Intellectual Property

- All products, algorithms, and code developed at ZURU Melon are company property unless otherwise specified.
- Employees may not use company IP for personal projects or outside business interests.

## 7. Use of Company Technology

- Use company-provided tools and software for business purposes only.
- Protect devices with strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
- Immediate reporting of lost devices or suspicious incidents is mandatory.

## 8. Social Media & Public Statements

- Do not disclose confidential company or client information online.
- Only authorized spokespersons may speak on behalf of ZURU Melon.
- Employees are encouraged to share public, pre-approved company content.

## 9. Continuous Learning & Innovation

- Employees are encouraged and supported to learn new AI technologies and methodologies.
- ZURU Melon sponsors professional development, conferences, and relevant certifications.

## 10. Reporting Issues

- Employees can report misconduct, security, or ethical concerns anonymously via the company intranet.
- Reports will be handled promptly and investigatively, with no retaliation.
